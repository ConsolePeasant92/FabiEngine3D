# Sounds

## 1. Types

- [Sound2D](SOUND2D.md)
- [Sound3D](SOUND3D.md)

## 2. Properties

- **Sound2D Channels**: the amount of allocated audio channels for 2D sounds
  - Type: `integer`
  - Constraints: between `64` and `512`
- **Sound3D Channels**: the amount of allocated audio channels for 3D sounds
  - Type: `integer`
  - Constraints: between `64` and `512`
